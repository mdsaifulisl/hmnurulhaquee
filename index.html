About Me

I am H.M. Nurul Haque a dedicated entrepreneur and owner of Rachna Sharee Bitan , a wholesale business that offers quality products at competitive prices. My entrepreneurial journey has started in 1993. My entrepreneurial spirit and commitment to customer satisfaction have made me a respected figure in the industry.

Beyond business endeavors, I am deeply committed to my community. I am actively engaged in social work by being involved with various organizations. I am playing a key leadership role as Joint General Secretary of the South Banasree Plot Welfare Association and General Secretary of the South Banasree Brihottor Dhaka Forum. My passion for social welfare drives me to work toward the betterment of my community, striving to make a positive impact through my efforts.

Social Activities

Founder President

In 2008, in the newspaper of Dakshin Gobdha, Durgapur, Aditmari, Lalmonirhat, we see that the Muslim people there converted from Islam to Christianity with the financial support of Christians. When it comes to our attention, we go there and see the truth. When I was the Banga Bazar Masjid Committee president after Satya Jana, we all discussed that a madrasa should be built in that area. Then people's ideas about Islam will change and no one will leave Islam and no one will accept Christianity. Then this madrasa was established and I was appointed as the founding president.
